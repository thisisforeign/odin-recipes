Buah Keluak

Description

Buah keluak is like a cross between a dry stew and a curry. There are so many dishes in South East Asia that are not actually curries but what else are you going to call them? Like Ayam Masak Kicap.

So for simplicity, let's call it a curry. Of sorts.

The dish itself is made up of chicken and buak keluak, the aforementioned seeds, cooked with a highly aromatic spice paste which is homemade, naturally.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buah Keluak
  • The Filling
  • The Spice Paste
  • Onions (Bawang Besar)
  • Red Chillies (Chilli Merah)
  • Galangal (Lengkuas)
  • Lemongrass (Serai)
  • Turmeric (Kunyit)
  • Shrimp Paste (Belacan)
  • Candlenuts (Buah Keras, Kukui Nuts)
  • Tamarind (Asam Jawa)
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves
